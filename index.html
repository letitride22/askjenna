How it works

Four steps from
chaos to perfect trip

No more endless group chats. AskJenna turns everyone's wishlist into a single, AI-optimized plan — and keeps working once you land.

01 👥

Create your crew

Start a trip in seconds and share a simple invite link. No app download required for guests. Set your destination, travel dates, and a rough budget range — or let Jenna suggest a destination based on everyone's interests.

Shareable invite link No download needed Budget ranges Anonymous preferences
02 🎯

Share your vibe

Each group member independently picks from 60+ interest categories — food, nightlife, museums, hiking, concerts, spas, sports, and more. Rate how important each is. Nobody sees each other's answers until Jenna compiles them.

60+ categories Priority weighting Dietary preferences Accessibility needs
03 ✦

Jenna works her magic

AskJenna's AI scores every activity, restaurant, and experience against the whole group's preferences. It finds what satisfies the most people, surfaces smart compromises, and shows each traveler their personal satisfaction score.

Group match score Per-person fit % Smart compromise logic Veto & re-suggest
04 📍

Live discovery

Once you land, Jenna becomes your real-time guide. She surfaces nearby hidden gems, live events, concerts, sports games, and flash deals — even adapting to the weather. "It's raining — here are 5 incredible indoor things nearby."

Real-time location Live event feeds Weather adaptation Flash deals

Platform features

Everything a group
of travelers needs

Built from the ground up for how real people actually plan and experience travel together.

✦

AI interest matching

Jenna weighs each person's preferences against 10,000+ activities to find the plan that makes the most people happiest.

📍

Real-time discovery

Live location-aware suggestions once you're at your destination. Concerts tonight, a farmers market two blocks away, current wait times.

🗳

Group voting & veto

Anyone can suggest changes, vote on alternatives, or veto an activity. Jenna instantly re-optimizes. No drama, just decisions.

💰

Expense splitting

Track shared costs, split bills, and settle up — all inside AskJenna. No more Venmo screenshots three days after the trip.
